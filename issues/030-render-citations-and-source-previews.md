## What to build

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Citations show document title, page, section, and source label when available.
- [ ] Table and figure previews can be opened or inspected from the answer.
- [ ] Preview loading and missing-preview states are handled.
- [ ] Citations map to answer claims or evidence items.
- [ ] Frontend tests cover citation and preview rendering.
